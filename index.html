Klopp and Wijnaldum shortlisted for Premier League awards

Jurgen Klopp and Georginio Wijnaldum have today been shortlisted for the Premier League's respective Manager of the Month and Player of the Month awards for March - and fans can try to help them win by voting now.

Klopp faces competition from Sam Allardyce (Crystal Palace), Antonio Conte (Chelsea), Eddie Howe (Bournemouth), Mauricio Pochettino (Tottenham Hotspur) and Craig Shakespeare (Leicester City).

Wijnaldum is up against Artur Boruc and Josh King (both Bournemouth), Romelu Lukaku (Everton), Nathan Redmond (Southampton) and Mamadou Sakho (Crystal Palace).

The winners will be decided by a combination of a Premier League panel and a public poll, which is worth 10 per cent of the overall vote.

About LFC

Liverpool Football Club is a professional association football club based in Liverpool, Merseyside, England.

They compete in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. The club has won 5 European Cups, 3 UEFA Cups, 3 UEFA Super Cups, 18 League titles, 7 FA Cups, a record 8 League Cups, and 15 FA Community Shields.
